Bookly App

“If you don't like to read, you haven't found the right book.” And what better way to do so, than by using Bookly? The Bookly app, intended for desktop use, utilizes the Google Books API to to search through millions of entries, to find the right match for you. Besides the search term, you have the option to filter the results by category, page count and publication date. So what’s stopping you? Give it a try!
