Grolly Weine

The winery Grolly, located in the western part of the “Weinviertel”, has been in family possession since 1878. After the siblings, Maria and Sebastian Grolly, took over the business, their wines have become sought by wine connoisseurs across Europe. The new visual appearance holds true to their un-compromising demand for no-frills quality.

While navigating through the website, the smooth page transitions create a seamless user experience. This was achieved using the Barba.js library.

The website is fully responsive and looks great no matter what device it is viewed on.
